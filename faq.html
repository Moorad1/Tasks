FAQ

Q: What is Tasks?

Tasks is a simple, powerful todo list website, It is used to put all of the future plans like Finish School Assignment or a place to put all of your ideas that you want to work on then when you are free you can look up what to do next.


Q: Do I have to buy or pay for anything?

No, Tasks is completely free with no ads, no upgrading plans and no locked features. If you want to support me please consider a Donation


Q: Is there a phone application version of Tasks?

No, But there is a plan for making an Android SDK app that is going to be released sometime soon.


Q: Does it work Offline?

Unfortunately No, But when an android SDK app releases it will have Offline capability.


Q: Can I use it on my phone?

Yes, you can use any browser in your phone and it will work correctly and this site is smartphone compatible.
